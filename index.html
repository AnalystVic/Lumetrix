About Lumetrix Global Institute

Building Africa's most impactful online learning community.

Our Mission

Africa's #1 Online Learning Platform by 2030

Founded in 2020, Lumetrix Global Institute emerged from a simple but powerful observation: talented professionals across Africa lacked access to quality, affordable, and practical technical education.

Our founders — a team of data scientists, engineers, and educators — set out to build a platform that doesn't just teach skills, but transforms careers and communities across the continent and beyond.
